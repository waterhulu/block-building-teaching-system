# 古代天文 - 双语词汇表

## 核心词汇

| 中文 | English | 发音 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|------|
| 天文 | astronomy | /əˈstrɒnəmi/ | Study astronomy. |
| 星星 | star | /stɑː/ | Stars twinkle at night. |
| 月亮 | moon | /muːn/ | The moon is bright. |
| 太阳 | sun | /sʌn/ | The sun gives us light. |
| 浑天仪 | armillary sphere | /əˈmɪləri sfɪə/ | Ancient astronomical instrument. |
| 日晷 | sundial | /ˈsʌndaɪəl/ | Tell time with sundial. |
| 宇宙 | universe | /ˈjuːnɪvɜːs/ | The universe is vast. |
| 银河 | Milky Way | /ˈmɪlki weɪ/ | See the Milky Way. |
