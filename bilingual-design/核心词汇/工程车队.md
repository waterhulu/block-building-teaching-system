# 工程车队 - 双语词汇表

## 核心词汇

| 中文 | English | 发音 | 例句 |
|------|---------|------|------|
| 挖掘机 | excavator | /ˈekskəveɪtə/ | The excavator digs. |
| 起重机 | crane | /kreɪn/ | The crane lifts heavy things. |
| 翻斗车 | dump truck | /dʌmp trʌk/ | The dump truck carries dirt. |
| 搅拌车 | mixer | /ˈmɪksə/ | The mixer mixes concrete. |
| 推土机 | bulldozer | /ˈbʊldəʊzə/ | The bulldozer pushes earth. |
| 压路机 | roller | /ˈrəʊlə/ | The roller flattens roads. |
| 卡车 | truck | /trʌk/ | The truck delivers materials. |
| 工人 | worker | /ˈwɜːkə/ | The worker operates the machine. |
